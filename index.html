Project Name

Tea
Journey

An interactive learning website transforming Chinese tea culture into a structured, learner-centered experience.

Launch Project

Overview

Tea Journey is an interactive digital environment exploring computational problem spaces. It promotes self-directed learning and scalable experiences through a comprehensive exploration of traditional Chinese tea culture.

Gamification & Simulation

Transforms traditional culture into a structured, learner-centered experience through engaging simulations and guided exploration.

AI-Supported Learning

Developed contextual multimedia content and API-supported knowledge exploration to adapt to learners' inquiries in real-time.

01 — LEARNING EXPERIENCE

Guided Exploration

Interactive learning features and contextual multimedia content intuitively guide learners through the rich history and aesthetic procedures of Chinese tea.

02 — INTERACTION

Procedural Simulation

Gamification elements and interactive simulations support procedural knowledge acquisition, allowing users to practice the sequence and art of tea brewing virtually.

Applied Principles

Improving engagement and supporting procedural knowledge acquisition.

Scaffolding

Structured the learning experience with step-by-step guidance, gradually fading support as learners acquire mastery over the tea procedures.

Decomposition

Broke down complex cultural narratives and brewing steps into manageable, interactive nodes to significantly reduce cognitive load.

Experiential Learning

Fostered learning through doing by utilizing interactive simulations that allow learners to practice, make choices, and reflect on outcomes.
